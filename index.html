Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2026

"Focus Reader operates completely offline. We do not collect, store, or transmit any user data. Saved articles are kept locally on your device."

1. Information Collection and Use

Focus Reader is built with a zero-telemetry architecture. We do not require account creation, and we do not collect any personal data, browsing history, or analytical metrics. The extension processes text structures directly inside your local browser memory space.

2. Local Storage Implementation

Any articles or preferences saved by the user are stored locally within the browser's sandboxed storage mechanism (such as chrome.storage.local or IndexedDB). This data is completely isolated and cannot be accessed by external parties or servers.

3. Third-Party Services

The extension does not communicate with third-party APIs, analytic software, or external tracking pixels. Your reading behavior remains 100% private to you.

4. Compliance and Safety

This policy is designed to strictly comply with the Google Chrome Web Store Developer Terms, ensuring high standards of user trust and data safety.
